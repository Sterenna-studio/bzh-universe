Sniky – The Shadow of BZH Chronicles

Role:

Class: Assassin furtif

Function: Espion, éclaireur, et saboteur

Allegiance: BZH Power

Companion: A loyal lieutenant handling external targets.

Description:

Sniky is an enigmatic figure, always dressed in dark attire tailored for stealth. His mask, equipped with thermal and night vision devices, enhances his anonymity and efficiency in the dark. He moves with fluidity and silence, reminiscent of a predator on the hunt.

Skills:

  • Infiltration: Mastery in sneaking past defenses and bypassing security systems.
  • Stealth Combat: Expert in quick, silent eliminations using energy blades.
  • Intelligence Gathering: Skilled in collecting critical information and manipulating enemy perceptions.

Equipment:

  • Tactical stealth suit with sound-absorbing technology.
  • Retractable energy blades for swift kills.
  • Technological devices like sensor jammers and reconnaissance drones.
  • Secondary weapons: adhesive bombs and tranquilizer darts.

Backstory:

Born in the underbelly of a cyberpunk metropolis, Sniky learned to survive by exploiting his environment and remaining invisible. Recruited by MutenRock for his ingenuity and mastery of stealth, he has become an indispensable member of BZH Power.

Links with Other Members:

  • MutenRock: Sniky respects MutenRock's strategic brilliance and follows his orders with precision.
  • Aligax: A tense relationship; Sniky admires Aligax's strength but fears her impulsiveness.
  • Spirit: They collaborate well during missions despite their differing approaches.
  • Dr. Sorn: Sniky remains cautious of Dr. Sorn's technological prowess and potential overreach.
  • Gabilone: A natural partnership; Sniky infiltrates, and Gabilone hacks.

Quotes:

  • "I’m not here to talk. My silence is already too loud."
  • "The shadow never betrays… as long as it isn’t illuminated."
